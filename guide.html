your eye shape

Not all lashes are created equal. Build perfect lashes by learning what type of eye you have and what suits them the best.

ROUND

Your eyes are large, prominent and circular. You can usually see the whites of your eyes all the way around the cornea.

ALMOND

Your eye tapers to a point by the tear duct and at the outer eye.The outer part of the cornea is generally hidden by your top and bottom eyelids.

DOWNTURNED

The outer corners of your eyes are lower than the inner corner, giving it a slight droop.

HOODED

Your eye has a fold of skin that hangs over the natural crease, which makes your eyelid appear to retract into the crease when your eyes are open.

MONOLID

Your eyelids are flat and smooth on the surface and generally do not have a crease

PROMINENT

Prominent eyes give the appearance of projected lids in the eye socket area
